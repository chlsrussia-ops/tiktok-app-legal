2. Information We Collect

When you register for an account or contact us, we may collect:

  • Communication data: Information you provide when contacting our support team.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

2.3 Data from Third-Party Platforms

Our Service accesses publicly available data from social media platforms, including TikTok, through their official APIs. This data includes:

  • Public video metadata: Video titles, descriptions, hashtags, view counts, like counts, share counts, and comment counts.
  • Public profile information: Usernames, display names, profile descriptions, verification status, and public profile links (accessed via TikTok's user.info.profile scope).
  • Video lists: Publicly available video listings from user profiles (accessed via TikTok's video.list scope).

Important: We only access data that is publicly available through official API endpoints. We do not access private messages, private account information, non-public videos, or any other restricted content. We do not require end users of third-party platforms to log in or authenticate through our Service.
